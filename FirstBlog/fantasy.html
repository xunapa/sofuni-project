Are we up for a Fight of the Monsters?

The former UFC heavyweight champion has been linked with a mega fight with Jones for several months. The wheels for the contest seemed to be well and truly in motion after Jones reclaimed the light-heavyweight title from Daniel Cormier at UFC 214 with a vicious third-round KO victory and promptly called out the WWE superstar. However, Jones' positive pre-fight drug test, which discovered traces of the anabolic steroid Turanibol in his system, well and truly put paid to those plans. Despite the fact Jones is likely to receive a ban for his second anti-doping infringement, many believe the New York native could very well fight Lesnar in the near future.
